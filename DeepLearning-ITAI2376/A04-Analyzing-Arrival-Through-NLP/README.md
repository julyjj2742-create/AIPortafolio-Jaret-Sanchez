# A04 - Analyzing "Arrival" Through the Lens of NLP

## Overview
In this assignment, I analyzed the movie Arrival by looking at its communication challenges through the perspective of Natural Language Processing (NLP). The film follows Dr. Louise Banks as she tries to understand the language of extraterrestrial beings, and throughout the story, we see how complicated real communication can be, and how issues like ambiguity, cultural interpretation, and symbol grounding make the process even harder.

## Purpose
The purpose of this assignment was to take a fictional scenario and analyze it using real NLP ideas. This helped me understand how language, meaning, and interpretation are not just technical problems but also human ones. The movie gave me a way to explore topics like ambiguity, cultural differences, and nonlinear communication in a more visual and relatable way.
